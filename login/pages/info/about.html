About CivicConnect

Empowering Communities Through Technology

Project Overview

CivicConnect is a community engagement platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and local authorities. Our platform enables users to:

  • Report and track community issues
  • Participate in fundraising campaigns
  • Engage with local authorities
  • Stay informed about community developments

Why We Chose This Project

We recognized the need for a digital platform that could:

  • Improve communication between citizens and authorities
  • Streamline the process of reporting and resolving community issues
  • Promote transparency and accountability
  • Foster community engagement and participation

About Us

We are a team of Computer Science students from Benazir Bhutto Shaheed University Lyari, Batch 13 (2025). Our team is dedicated to using technology to create positive social impact in our communities.
